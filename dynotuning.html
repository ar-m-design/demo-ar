Available Tuning

Economy Tuning

Our economy ECU software improves fuel efficiency. Increasing the vehicles torque reduces gear changes and accelerator pedal movement thus resulting in improved MPG and driveability.

Stage 1 Tuning

Designed for standard, unmodified vehicles. Increases power and torque while also improving fuel efficiency.

Stage 2 Tuning

Bigger increase in power and torque than Stage 1 tuning. Hardware upgrades are required before a Stage 2 can be applied.

Stage 3 / Custom Tuning

For vehicles with numerous hardware additions, rolling road tuning is required.
