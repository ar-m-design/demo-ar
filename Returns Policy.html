Returns Policy


UNDER 14 DAYS

● You can return your goods for a full refund or exchange as long as you inform us of your intention to return the goods and do so within 14 days, starting from the day you receive your goods.
● All return costs are down to the buyer, it is recommended that you return your goods using a trackable method with the appropriate insurance.
● Once you have informed us of your intention to return the goods and a returns number has been issued, please ensure that you return the items within 14 days.
● Please ensure that the parcel has the returns number marked clearly.
● Items need to be returned in "as new" condition in their original packaging, used or damaged products will be subject to a 25% handling fee.


AFTER 14 DAYS

● We will not offer a refund after 14 days of you receiving your delivery unless your product is faulty.


EXCLUSIONS - products we can't offer returns on

● Special order items.
● Custom items.
● Products that include software which require activation.


IF YOUR PRODUCT BECOMES FAULTY

In most cases we find products with faults can be fixed with some basic troubleshooting, so to prevent additional inconvenience to you, we ask that you contact us and a member of staff will guide you through some troubleshooting before returning your items.


UNDER 30 DAYS

● If your items become faulty within 30 days of purchase you are entitled to a full refund or exchange.
● Please send the items back with a cover note including a description of the fault and your original order number.
● Once we have fully inspected the item and the fault has been confirmed, we will process the refund or exchange including the cost of returning the item (if the costs are reasonable).
● If during our testing we cannot find a fault, no refund will be given and you will be charged for delivery of the item back to you.


AFTER 30 DAYS

● After 30 days your faulty items may still be repaired or replaced within the manufacturers warranty, we will be happy to assist you with this. Please contact us and a member of staff will advise you on the best way to get your items repaired or replaced.
